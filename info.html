Assumptions & Methodology

Source notes

This project was originally documented in LegendKeeper and exported as static HTML. The dataset represents suburb-level aggregates rather than individual trips.

Commute calculations

  • Commute times are averaged per suburb and transport mode.
  • Public transport and driving are treated independently.
  • Suburbs without public transport commute data were excluded.

Visualisation choices

  • Charts are limited to suburbs within 20 km of the CBD.
  • Suburbs are ordered by CBD distance ascending.
  • Lines connect suburbs to show trend rather than routes.
