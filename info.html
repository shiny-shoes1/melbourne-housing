Assumptions & Methodology

Source

This project was documented in LegendKeeper and exported as static HTML. Data represents suburb-level aggregates.

Commute calculations

  • Commute times averaged per suburb and transport mode.
  • Public transport and driving calculated separately.
  • Suburbs without PT commute data excluded.

Charts

  • Scatter plots (no connecting lines).
  • CBD distance limited to 20 km.
  • Hover tooltips show suburb names.
